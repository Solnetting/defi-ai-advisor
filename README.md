# DeFi AI Advisor — Solana Portfolio Intelligence

Most Solana users leave yield on the table. Not because they don't care — because no tool shows them the cost of doing nothing.

DeFi AI Advisor analyzes your on-chain behavior and tells you exactly what your wallet reveals: idle SOL earning 0%, staking positions underperforming the market, and yield opportunities you're missing right now.

## Core Capabilities

### Idle Capital Detection
Scans your wallet for unstaked or underutilized SOL and surfaces the real cost of inaction — not as a percentage, but as money left behind.

### Yield Comparison Engine
Side-by-side analysis of Jito, Marinade, and native staking — factoring APY, liquidity, and risk — so you don't have to visit 10 dashboards to make one decision.

### Opportunity Cost Framing
Instead of "Jito = 6.8% APY", we tell you: "You are missing ~1.3% yield annually on your idle SOL."

### Risk Scoring
Every position is evaluated for risk level — so you know not just what you're earning, but what you're exposed to.

### Growth Potential Based on Your Position
See projected growth based on your actual current holdings — not generic market benchmarks.

### Portfolio Rebalancing Recommendations
AI-generated rebalancing suggestions tailored to your wallet: what to move, where to move it, and why.

### Three-Level Forecast
Conservative, moderate, and aggressive scenarios for your portfolio — so you can plan based on your risk appetite.

### AI Chat Advisor
An active conversational AI tool that answers your questions, explains your positions, and gives real-time advice based on your wallet data.
